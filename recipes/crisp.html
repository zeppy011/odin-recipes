Ingredients

  • 2 cans (21 ounces each) apple pie filling
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • Vanilla ice cream, optional

Steps

  1. Spread apples evenly in lightly greased 13x9x2-inch (3-quart rectangular) baking dish. Drizzle juice over top.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine 1/2 cup sugar and the cinnamon; sprinkle over apples. In same bowl, combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, and the salt. Stir in softened butter until crumbly. Sprinkle evenly over apple mixture.
  3. Bake in a 350 degree F oven for 40 to 45 minutes or until top is lightly golden and apples are tender. Serve warm with ice cream, if you like. Makes 8 servings.
